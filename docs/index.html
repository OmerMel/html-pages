Italy

Italy, a country located in Southern Europe, is known for its rich history, art, architecture, and delicious food. From the Roman ruins to the Renaissance cities, Italy has a lot to offer. The country is also famous for its beautiful lakes, mountains, and coastal towns.

Top Attractions

Colosseum

This ancient amphitheater is one of the most iconic landmarks in Rome, Italy. (41.8902, 12.4903)

Leaning Tower of Pisa

This famous tower is known for its unintended tilt, and is a must-visit attraction in Pisa, Italy. (43.7229, 10.3942)

Grand Canal

This beautiful canal runs through the heart of Venice, Italy, and is lined with stunning architecture and historic landmarks. (45.4347, 12.3356)

Roman Forum

This ancient Roman site is a collection of ruins, including temples, basilicas, and markets. (41.8903, 12.4853)

Pompeii

This ancient Roman city was frozen in time by the devastating volcanic eruption of Mount Vesuvius, and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site. (40.7517, 14.4844)
