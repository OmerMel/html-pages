Local Attractions

  • Colosseum in Rome: This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and a must-visit attraction.
  • Leaning Tower of Pisa: This famous tower is a unique and fascinating sight to behold.
  • Canal Grande in Venice: Take a romantic gondola ride or stroll along the canal to admire the beautiful architecture and scenery.
