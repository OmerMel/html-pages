Discover Italy

Explore the beauty of Italy with our recommendations for local attractions and a map to guide you.

Local Attractions

  • Rome: Explore the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Pantheon.
  • Venice: Take a gondola ride through the Grand Canal, visit St. Mark's Basilica, and get lost in the narrow streets.
  • Florence: Visit the Uffizi Gallery, Duomo, and Ponte Vecchio.
